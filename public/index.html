Intro

As you know, my name is Marta. I was born in Mataró, Barcelona, in 1989.

Did my studies in UAB about labour relations. Started to work in HR in 2014, so six years ago. After being in quarantine at home I decided to undertake a new project, I wanted to teach myself some web development.

I started with some Free Code Camp exercises, and this website is my way to put what I've learned in practice. I am far from considering myself a frontend developer, but I am enjoying this learning journey. My intention here is just to practice, learn and above everything else, have fun.

So, welcome to my playground.
