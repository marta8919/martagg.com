About

I started with some Free Code Camp exercises, Udacity courses and Scrimba tutorials, and this website is my way to put what I've learned in practice. I am far from considering myself a web developer, but I am enjoying this learning journey. My intention here is just to practice, learn and above everything else, have fun.

So, welcome to my playground.
